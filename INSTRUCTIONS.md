# User Instructions

## Property Investment Calculator

This calculator helps users assess mortgage payments, rental income, property cash flow, and investment performance across multiple countries.

## Basic Rule

**Only complete the blue cells.**

Blue cells are user input cells. All other cells may contain formulas, calculations, or protected assumptions and should not be changed unless you understand the model structure.

## Step 1: Enter Property Details

Complete the blue input cells for the property being assessed, including:

- Purchase price
- Deposit amount or deposit percentage
- Mortgage amount
- Mortgage term
- Mortgage interest rate
- Repayment type, where applicable
- Expected rental income, if the property will be rented out

## Step 2: Enter Mortgage Assumptions

Enter the relevant mortgage assumptions in the blue cells, including:

- Interest rate
- Loan term
- Repayment frequency
- Any fees or charges included in the calculation

The calculator will then estimate mortgage payments based on the inputs provided.

## Step 3: Enter Rental Assumptions

If the property will be rented out, complete the relevant rental input cells, including:

- Monthly rent
- Vacancy allowance
- Management fees
- Maintenance allowance
- Insurance
- Service charges or ground rent, where applicable
- Other recurring costs

## Step 4: Enter Country Rules

Before relying on the results, review and complete the country-specific rules section.

Country rules may include:

- Currency
- Purchase taxes
- Stamp duty or transfer taxes
- Legal fees
- Agent fees
- Ownership costs
- Rental tax assumptions
- Mortgage-related fees
- Any other local property costs

This calculator works across multiple countries, but users must make sure the local rules entered are correct and current.

## Step 5: Review the Summary

Once the blue cells and country rules have been completed, review the summary outputs.

The workbook may show estimates such as:

- Mortgage payments
- Total purchase costs
- Monthly rental profit or loss
- Annual cash flow
- Gross rental yield
- Net rental yield
- Investment return indicators
- Affordability outputs

## Important Notes

- Results are estimates only.
- Do not overwrite formulas.
- Check all country rules before using the outputs.
- Mortgage rates, taxes, fees, rents, and property laws can change.
- This calculator is not financial, mortgage, tax, legal, or investment advice.
